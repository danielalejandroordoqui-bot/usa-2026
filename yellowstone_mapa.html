Día 12 — West Yellowstone → Lower Loop → Gardiner
Salida Ho Hum al amanecer. Madison Valley (bisontes, cisnes) → Old Faithful → West Thumb Geyser Basin → Fishing Bridge → Canyon Village → subir a Gardiner por Mammoth. Primera noche en el Super 8.
Día 13 — Northern Range: Lamar Valley y Cooke City
Salida 5:45 AM. Mammoth → Tower Junction → Tower Fall → Lamar Valley (lobos, grizzly, pronghorns — ir más allá de Soda Butte Creek) → Cooke City / Silver Gate. Regreso: volver vía Canyon → Norris → Mammoth (loop completo, 145 millas, 4h 53min). Autos con telescopios en la ruta = siempre frenar.
Día 14 — Canyon, Hayden Valley y salida sur a Jackson
Salida ~6 AM. Mammoth → Canyon Village (Gran Cañón, Artist Point) → Hayden Valley (manadas de bisontes, grizzly) → West Thumb → South Entrance → Grand Teton NP → Jackson al mediodía.
